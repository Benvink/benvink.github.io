What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important.

A responsive website is one that is designed for many devices, irrespective of their size. Its important to have responsiveness websites because it allows anyone to access your website on any type of device (Desktop, Mobile, Tablet, TVs or Smart watches

What mobile first design is, and why it's important.

Designing a website with mobile first in mind is important because its easier to resize and reshape images when they get larger. By designing with mobile in mind, it ensures your website will render correctly on desktops too

What frameworks are, and their pros and cons.

Frameworks are a skeleton to your website, often code is written for you and you can tweak the odd code to make it your own. the limitations with frameworks is that it doesn't teach you how to develop, but rather gives you the coding. apart from this, it can be difficult to make the website look the way you want it to look without starting completely from scratch

What a wireframe is and why we use it.

A wireframe allows us to visualise our website prior to us writing code. its a lot easier when you have a visual reference prior to coding, otherwise you end up redoing code without an end goal in mind
